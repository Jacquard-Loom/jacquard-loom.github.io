Jacquard Loom

weaving open source algebraic patterns

Software development is a creative art. As Ada Lovelace said, “the Analytical Engine weaves algebraic patterns just as the Jacquard loom weaves flowers and leaves.” It's also a communal activity. We create and push boundaries together. Jacqurd Loom is an open source organization with these two truths at its core. It's a place where we can learn & grow; it's a place where we do what's next.

Open Source Collaboration

The heart of Jacquard Loom is its focus on free and open source software. There are two types of projects: organization driven & community driven. Organization driven projects are those that the founding team of Jacquard Loom lead. These projects currently include a web accessibility audit web app, an Express.js MVC generator, and a simple static site generator.

Community driven projects are those that anyone wishes to open up to the Jacquard Loom community for development and collaboration. The community itself will discuss project proposals and will decide which projects to take on in a transparent & democratic manner. If you'd like to join the community or submit a proposal, email vgabruzzo@gmail.com.

The goal here is essentially to build beautiful software and to give back to the open source community.
